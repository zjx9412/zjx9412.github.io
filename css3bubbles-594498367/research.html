Research interest

My research is focused on investigating mantle convection and its connection to and impact on deep earth structures, surface observations and evolution of Earth's interior. I look into them through numerical methods to model fluid dynamic process within Earth's mantle, which acts like fluid. In collaboration with seismologists, geochemists and mineral physicsists, I come up with and test hypotheses on energy interaction and chemical transportation within Earth's interior caused by mantle convection.
